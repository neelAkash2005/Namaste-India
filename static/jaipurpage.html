Explore Jaipur

The Pink City of India—rich in royal palaces, vibrant bazaars, and historic forts.

Main Attractions

Hawa Mahal

The iconic “Palace of Winds,” famous for its intricate lattice windows and royal history.

Amber Fort

A majestic hilltop fort blending Hindu and Mughal architecture, with panoramic views of Jaipur.

Jantar Mantar

An astronomical observatory built in the 18th century, showcasing remarkable historical instruments.

Must-Try Foods

Daal Baati Churma

A traditional Rajasthani meal of baked wheat balls, spiced lentils, and sweet crumbled churma.

Ghevar

A crispy sweet dessert soaked in sugar syrup, often enjoyed during festivals like Teej.

Pyaz Ki Kachori

Spicy onion-stuffed pastry, a popular street food snack in Jaipur.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal with pleasant weather for sightseeing. Attend the Jaipur Literature Festival in January to experience the city’s cultural vibrancy.
