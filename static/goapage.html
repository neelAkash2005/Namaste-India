Explore Goa

India’s beach paradise—sun-kissed shores, vibrant nightlife, and a blend of Portuguese charm.

Main Attractions

Baga Beach

One of Goa’s most popular beaches, known for water sports, shacks, and lively evenings.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage Site housing the relics of St. Francis Xavier, showcasing baroque architecture.

Fort Aguada

A 17th-century Portuguese fort overlooking the Arabian Sea, offering panoramic views and history.

Must-Try Foods

Goan Fish Curry

A tangy coconut-based curry with fresh fish, perfectly paired with steamed rice.

Prawn Balchao

A spicy and flavorful pickle-style prawn dish influenced by Portuguese cuisine.

Bebinca

Goa’s signature layered dessert made of coconut milk, eggs, and ghee—sweet and decadent.

Best Time to Visit

November to February is perfect for pleasant weather and beach activities. Visit in late December to enjoy Goa Carnival and lively New Year celebrations.
