Explore Mumbai

The city of dreams—where Bollywood glamour meets colonial charm and a bustling waterfront.

Main Attractions

Gateway of India

An iconic waterfront arch built in 1924, perfect for sunrise views and boat rides to Elephanta Island.

Marine Drive

The famous “Queen’s Necklace,” a 3.6-km boulevard along the Arabian Sea ideal for evening strolls.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus

A UNESCO World Heritage railway station blending Victorian Gothic architecture with Indian influences.

Must-Try Foods

Vada Pav

Mumbai’s iconic street snack—spicy potato fritter in a soft bun, served with chutneys.

Pav Bhaji

A buttery mix of spiced vegetables served with soft buns, loved by locals and visitors alike.

Bhel Puri

A crunchy, tangy snack of puffed rice, vegetables, and chutneys found along Mumbai’s beaches.

Best Time to Visit

November to February offers pleasant weather for sightseeing and beach walks. Visit during Ganesh Chaturthi (Aug–Sept) to witness grand celebrations across the city.
