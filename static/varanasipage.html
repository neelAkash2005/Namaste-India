Explore Varanasi

The spiritual heart of India—where ancient ghats, timeless rituals, and sacred Ganga meet.

Main Attractions

Dashashwamedh Ghat

Witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti every evening on this vibrant riverfront ghat.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the holiest Shiva temples, a must-visit for devotees and history lovers alike.

Sarnath

The site of Buddha’s first sermon, featuring serene stupas and ancient ruins.

Must-Try Foods

Banarasi Paan

A traditional betel leaf preparation—sweet, fragrant, and symbolic of the city’s culture.

Kachori Sabzi

Flaky fried bread with spicy potato curry, the quintessential Varanasi breakfast.

Banarasi Lassi

Rich yogurt drink topped with malai and nuts, served in a traditional clay cup.

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers cool, pleasant weather. Experience the grand Dev Deepawali festival when thousands of diyas light up the ghats, creating a breathtaking spectacle.
