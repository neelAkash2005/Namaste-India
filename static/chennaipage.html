Explore Chennai

The gateway to South India—where ancient temples, vibrant culture, and golden beaches meet.

Main Attractions

Marina Beach

India’s longest urban beach, perfect for sunrise walks and evening breezes along the Bay of Bengal.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

A magnificent Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, rich in color and intricate carvings.

Fort St. George

Built in 1644 by the British, this historic fort houses a museum and remnants of colonial architecture.

Must-Try Foods

South Indian Filter Coffee

Strong, aromatic coffee brewed in the traditional style and served frothy in a steel tumbler.

Masala Dosa

A crisp fermented crepe stuffed with spiced potatoes, accompanied by sambar and chutneys.

Chettinad Chicken

A fiery chicken curry made with freshly ground spices—a specialty of Tamil Nadu cuisine.

Best Time to Visit

The best months are November to February, when the weather is cooler and pleasant. Experience the city’s culture during the vibrant Margazhi Music Season of December–January.
