Explore Delhi

The capital city of India—where history, politics, and culture coexist in a vibrant mix.

Main Attractions

Red Fort

The iconic 17th-century Mughal fort with stunning architecture and historical significance.

Qutub Minar

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the tallest brick minaret in the world, dating back to 1193 AD.

India Gate

A war memorial honoring Indian soldiers, set amidst lush lawns and fountains.

Must-Try Foods

Chole Bhature

Spicy chickpea curry served with deep-fried fluffy bread—a Delhi breakfast classic.

Butter Chicken

Rich, creamy chicken curry with tomato-based gravy—originated in Delhi and loved nationwide.

Dahi Bhalla

Soft lentil dumplings soaked in creamy yogurt, topped with tangy chutneys and spices, making it a refreshing street food delight.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for sightseeing in comfortable weather. Visit during Diwali or Republic Day to witness vibrant celebrations.
