Explore Shimla

The Queen of Hills—snow-capped peaks, pine forests, and charming colonial heritage await you.

Main Attractions

The Ridge

A spacious open street in the heart of Shimla, offering stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Jakhoo Temple

Dedicated to Lord Hanuman, this hilltop temple provides panoramic views of the entire city.

Kufri

A short drive from Shimla, famous for its skiing slopes, hiking trails, and breathtaking vistas.

Must-Try Foods

Chana Madra

A traditional Himachali dish made with chickpeas cooked in a creamy yogurt-based gravy.

Sidu

Fluffy steamed bread stuffed with a mixture of spices and nuts, often served with ghee.

Babru

A Himachali version of the kachori, filled with black gram paste and served hot.

Best Time to Visit

March to June is perfect for pleasant summer weather and sightseeing. For snow lovers, December to February brings beautiful winter landscapes and snowfall.
